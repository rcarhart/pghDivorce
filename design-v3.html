Questions?

Common things people ask before they submit a request.

Is PittsburghDivorce a law firm?

No. We're a consultation-request service. We connect people in the Pittsburgh area with qualified divorce attorneys. We don't give legal advice and we're not a law firm.

Will my information be kept private?

Yes. Complete confidentiality is something we take seriously. Your submission is never shared without your explicit consent. We don't sell leads to random third parties.

What if I'm not sure I want a divorce yet?

That's completely fine. Many people reach out while still in the early stages of thinking this through. An attorney can help you understand your options and what to expect — with no pressure to move forward.

Which parts of Pittsburgh do you cover?

We cover Allegheny, Washington, Westmoreland, Butler, and Beaver counties. If you're in another southwestern Pennsylvania county, reach out anyway — we may still be able to help.

Is there any cost to submit a request?

Zero. Submitting a request is free. Any fees for legal representation are between you and your attorney.
