Questions

You might be wondering…

We want you to feel completely comfortable before you reach out.

Is PittsburghDivorce a law firm?

No. We're a consultation-request service that connects people in the Pittsburgh area with qualified local divorce attorneys. We don't provide legal advice and are not a law firm.

Is my information confidential?

Absolutely. Everything you share is treated with complete confidentiality. We don't sell personal information or share it without your explicit consent.

What if I'm not ready to file yet?

You don't need to be. A consultation is just a conversation. Many people who reach out are still in the early stages of thinking things through. An attorney can help you understand your options with no pressure to move forward.

Which counties do you cover?

We cover Allegheny, Washington, Westmoreland, Butler, and Beaver counties in southwestern Pennsylvania. Reach out even if your county isn't listed — we may still be able to help.

Is there any cost to request a consultation?

No. Submitting your request is completely free. Any fees related to legal representation are a matter between you and the attorney you choose.
