Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

We want you to feel informed and comfortable before you submit anything.

Is PittsburghDivorce a law firm?

No. PittsburghDivorce is a consultation-request service that connects prospective clients with qualified Pittsburgh-area divorce attorneys. We are not a law firm and do not provide legal advice.

Is my information confidential?

Yes. Your submission is treated with complete confidentiality. We never sell or share your personal information without your explicit consent.

Do I have to be ready to file right now?

Absolutely not. Many people reach out while still exploring options. Whether you're just starting to consider divorce or already in the process, we can connect you with the right attorney for your stage.

Which counties do you serve?

We primarily serve Allegheny, Washington, Westmoreland, Butler, and Beaver counties — the core Pittsburgh metro area. Other southwestern Pennsylvania counties may be accommodated depending on attorney availability.

Is there any cost to submit a request?

No. Submitting a consultation request is completely free. Any fees associated with legal representation are a matter between you and the attorney you choose to retain.

Ready to take the first step?

Your consultation request is confidential, free, and carries no obligation.
