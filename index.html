How PittsburghDivorce works

The website is designed as a straightforward intake funnel: attract local search traffic, establish trust quickly, gather the right information, and package leads for attorneys who want serious Pittsburgh-area divorce prospects.

1

They see a professional Pittsburgh-focused brand and a clear message about confidential consultation requests.

2

FAQ

This section helps reduce hesitation, increase trust, and clarify expectations before someone submits a lead.

Is this a law firm?

PittsburghDivorce is positioned as a consultation-request website that helps connect prospective clients with attorneys. It is not itself a law firm.

Is my information confidential?

The site is designed to request sensitive information in a professional and private way, with clear language that sets expectations around contact and representation.

Do I have to be ready to file right now?

No. The lead form can be used by people who are just exploring, preparing to file, or already in process and looking for help.
